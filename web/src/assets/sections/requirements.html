Requirements

Here is a list of all supported platforms:

  • Windows: Windows 10 64bits, Windows Server 2016 64bits
  • MacOS: macOS 10.11 Yosemite & macOS 10.12 Sierra
  • Linux: Ubuntu 16.04 32/64bits

Here is a list of all supported databases through Wakanda drivers & connectors:

  • 4D v16 R3 and higher
  • MySQL 4.1 and higher
  • MSSQL 2005 and higher through ODBC protocol 3.8
Drivers and connectors are available in Enterprise Edition
